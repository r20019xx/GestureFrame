About SignVisionAI

SignVisionAI started as our CS5704 Team Project aimed to empower Deaf and hard‑of‑hearing communities by leveraging computer vision. Our goal is to let people sign freely in ASL and have our system translate it instantly into text.

Under the hood we’ve trained a YOLOv5 model on hundreds of labeled ASL signs. It runs real‑time in the browser via Django → REST → JavaScript, detecting hand gestures and returning the corresponding word.

Next up (outside course expectations):

  • Fine‑tune for continuous signing (multiple words in sequence)
  • Expand the vocabulary to include fingerspelling for names & uncommon words
  • Build a mobile‑friendly UI so you can sign on the go

Our team is passionate about making communication more accessible.
